The shift

Today it helps the crew. Over time, it does the collecting.

Right now a passenger pays and shows their phone; the crew checks it by eye. Botride replaces the squinting with a clear, shared view of who has paid — and builds, step by step, towards fare collection that runs on its own.

See who has paid

A live, ordered list of paid fares for the trip — by name and amount. No more reading messages off a passenger's phone.

The right fare, every leg

Fares follow the route. Each fare is counted once and can't be quietly reused on the next leg.

A record you can trust

Every trip leaves a clean record of what was collected — for the crew, the owner, and the SACCO.

No new hardware

It runs on the crew's own phone and the vehicle's own connection. Nothing to fit in the vehicle.

Works with M-Pesa

Passengers keep paying the way they already do. Botride organises the payments around that — it doesn't replace them.

Built for the road

Designed to keep working through patchy signal and busy stages — the everyday reality of a matatu route.

Who it's for

Everyone on the trip.

Passengers

Pay your fare and see it counted — no haggling, no showing your phone.

Crews

See who has paid at a glance, and spend the trip moving, not checking.

Owners & SACCOs

A trustworthy, per-trip record of fares collected across the fleet.
