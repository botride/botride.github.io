Who it's for

Everyone on the trip benefits.

Botride isn't built for one side of the fare. It makes the trip clearer for the passenger, easier for the crew, and accountable for the people who run the vehicles.

Passengers

Pay and go.

  • Pay your fare the way you already do.
  • See your fare counted — no haggling over the amount.
  • No need to wave your phone at the conductor.
Crews

Spend the trip moving.

  • A live list of who has paid, by name and amount.
  • Each fare counted once — nothing slips through twice.
  • Less time squinting at messages, more time working the stage.
Owners & SACCOs

Know what came in.

  • A clear, per-trip record of fares collected.
  • The same picture across every vehicle you run.
  • A foundation for oversight and fairer accounting.
